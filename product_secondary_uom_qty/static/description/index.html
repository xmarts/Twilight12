Stock in Different UOMs

Enable Different Unit of Measure

Enable "Some products may be sold/purchased in different unit of measures (advanced)". in Purchase Settings or Sales Settings

Stock Quantity in Secondary UOM

Stock qty on hand and Forecasted in secondary unit of measure. This will helps you to show in two UOMs. Eg: In Dozen and pieces, Kg and gm, Box and pieces etc.
